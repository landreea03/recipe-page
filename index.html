Simple Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, meats

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter/oil in a pan.
  3. Cook eggs evenly, add fillings.
  4. Fold omelette and serve hot.

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and egg, whisk until smooth.
  3. Cook on a non-stick pan until golden brown.
  4. Serve with syrup or fruits.

Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Carrots
  • Olive oil & vinegar

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Mix in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar.
  4. Toss and serve.
